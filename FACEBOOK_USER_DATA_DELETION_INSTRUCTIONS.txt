USER DATA DELETION INSTRUCTIONS
Barangay Bacayan Information System
https://barangaysystem.com

HOW TO REQUEST YOUR USER DATA

If you have signed up or logged in using Facebook, Google, or Apple, you can request a copy of your user data or request deletion of your account and associated data.

METHOD 1: REQUEST DATA VIA EMAIL

Send an email to: [YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS]
Subject: User Data Request - [Your Name]

Include the following information:
- Your full name
- Email address associated with your account
- Facebook User ID (if available)
- Type of request: "Data Export" or "Account Deletion"

METHOD 2: REQUEST DATA VIA IN-APP CONTACT FORM

1. Log in to your account at https://barangaysystem.com
2. Navigate to your Profile page
3. Use the contact form or support link
4. Select "Data Request" or "Account Deletion"
5. Provide your email address and reason for request

WHAT DATA WE STORE

When you sign up using Facebook, Google, or Apple, we store the following information:
- Your name
- Email address
- Profile picture/avatar (from your social account)
- Social provider (Facebook, Google, or Apple)
- Social provider ID
- Account creation date
- Any additional information you provide in your profile

HOW TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT AND DATA

To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

1. Send an email to: [YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS]
   Subject: Account Deletion Request - [Your Name]

2. Include the following:
   - Your full name
   - Email address associated with your account
   - Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
   - Facebook User ID (if available, for verification)

3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.

4. Once deleted, the following will be removed:
   - Your user account
   - All personal information
   - All associated data (clearances, complaints, reports, etc.)
   - Profile information
   - Social authentication links

NOTE: Some data may be retained for legal or administrative purposes as required by law, but will be anonymized and not associated with your identity.

VERIFICATION PROCESS

For security purposes, we may require additional verification:
- Confirmation email sent to your registered email address
- Identity verification questions
- Photo ID (for sensitive data requests)

RESPONSE TIME

- Data export requests: Processed within 30 days
- Account deletion requests: Processed within 30 days
- You will receive confirmation via email once your request is processed

CONTACT INFORMATION

For data requests or account deletion:
Email: [YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS]
Website: https://barangaysystem.com
Support Page: https://barangaysystem.com/support

ALTERNATIVE: DELETE VIA FACEBOOK

You can also request data deletion directly through Facebook:
1. Go to your Facebook Settings
2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
3. Find "Barangay Bacayan Information System"
4. Click "Remove" to disconnect the app
5. Your account will remain active, but Facebook login will be disabled

To completely delete your account and data, you must follow the email request process above.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

- Data is stored securely on our servers
- We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law
- You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
